Our creation and goal alongside SFRS is to uncover the mysteries of the "serpent folk", S.F. for short. S.F. artifacts were first unearthed at [REDACTED] site in, [REDACTED], Norway, by one Dr. Ingrid Solberg, on the 16th of June, 2007. June 30th, 2007, Dr. Solberg had sealed the deal with USEF, and officially gained the Head Researcher title at the newly created SFRS, a subsidiary of Division V. And on the 24th of September, 2007, the first expedition began. These expeditions are still going on today, as uncovering the ancient crypts, the language, which is named Ancient Sylphidian, and large amounts of writings in A.S. take a very long time to translate, due to such a large difference in the language of Ancient Sylphidian and say, modern English.
